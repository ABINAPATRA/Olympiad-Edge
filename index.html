❓ FAQs

Questions Parents
Always Ask

Can my child attend a trial class before enrolling? +
Yes! Every student is welcome to attend 1 free trial class before making any payment. This gives you and your child a chance to experience the teaching style, the class environment, and the level of attention — with absolutely no commitment or pressure to enrol.
Which classes can join this program? +
This program is exclusively for students in Class 3, Class 4, and Class 5. There is 1 batch per class, with a strict maximum of 5 students per batch.
How do I pay the fees? +
There is no payment needed when you register. Simply fill in the interest form, and Abina will personally call you within 24 hours. After your child attends the free trial class, Abina will privately share payment details with you directly. Fees are charged monthly in advance.
What is the homework and discipline policy? +
Students are expected to complete all assigned homework and worksheets on time. Students are allowed a maximum of 3 instances of incomplete homework per year. If a student fails to complete homework more than 3 times, they may be discontinued from the program.
What if my child does not understand a concept? +
That is exactly what this program is built for. No concept is skipped or rushed. Every topic is explained patiently and revisited as many times as needed until the student fully understands.
Why only 5 students per batch? +
Keeping the batch to just 5 students allows for genuine personal attention for every child — closer to one-on-one tutoring than group classes.
How are the classes conducted? +
All classes are conducted live on Google Meet — 2 sessions per week. Classes are fully interactive and not pre-recorded. All updates, worksheets, and class links are shared via the WhatsApp group.
Which Olympiad exams does this prepare for? +
The program prepares for major Olympiad platforms including SOF (IMO, NSO, IEO), SilverZone Olympiads, Maths Olympiad Foundation, and Government-organised Olympiads.
