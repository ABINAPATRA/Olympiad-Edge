❓ FAQs

Questions Parents
Always Ask

Which classes can join this program? +
This program is for students in Class 3, Class 4, and Class 5 only. We offer 1 dedicated batch per class, with a maximum of 5 students each for quality mentorship.
Why only 5 students per batch? +
With only 5 students, every child receives genuine personal attention — almost like one-on-one tutoring in a group setting. This is what has produced 30+ Gold Medals and International ranks among our students.
Does my child need prior Olympiad experience? +
No experience needed at all! We build from the foundation upward. Both first-time students and those who have previously appeared for Olympiads are welcome.
How are the classes conducted? +
All sessions are live on Google Meet — 2 sessions per week, each lasting 2 to 3 hours. Classes are interactive, not pre-recorded. Students can ask questions in real time. Google Meet links are shared via the WhatsApp group.
Which Olympiad exams does this cover? +
We prepare for SOF (IMO, NSO, IEO), SilverZone Olympiads, Maths Olympiad Foundation, and Government-organised Olympiads. Our students have won medals and achieved ranks across all these platforms over 8 years.
Are worksheets and mock tests really free? +
Yes — completely free and included in the program fee. Worksheets are shared after every session via WhatsApp. Full-length mock Olympiad tests are also provided at no extra charge.
Is the payment secure? +
Payments are processed via Razorpay — India's most trusted payment gateway. You can pay via UPI, debit card, credit card, or net banking. All transactions are 100% encrypted and secure.
